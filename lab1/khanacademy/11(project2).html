Beshbarmak

  • Time:in home conditions : 1-2 hour
  • Serves:3000tg
Ingredients Quantity
Horse meat,onion,patato 3-4kg meat,1 onion,5 patatos

Beshbarmak – cooking steps
Boil the meat (horse meat or beef) in cold water until tender.
Remove foam, add salt near the end.
Prepare dough with flour, water, and salt.
Roll out the dough and cut into pieces.
Boil the dough in the meat broth.
Cut the cooked meat into large pieces.
Place dough on a plate, add meat on top.
Pour onion with hot broth over the dish and serve hot.

Kuyrdaq

  • Time:in home conditions : 1 hour
  • Serves:3000tg
Ingredients Quantity
Horse or beef meat,onion,patato 1-2kg meat,1 onion,10 patatos

Kuyrdaq – cooking steps
Cut meat into small pieces.
Heat oil or fat in a pan.
Fry the meat until golden brown.
Add sliced onions and cook until soft.
Add potatoes (optional), salt, and pepper.
Cover and cook for 15–20 minutes.
Serve hot.

Palau Recipe

  • Time:in home conditions : 1 hour
  • Serves:3000tg
Ingredients Quantity
Horse or beef meat,onion,rice 1-2kg meat,1 onion,1kg rice

Palau – cooking steps
Cut meat into small pieces.
Heat oil or fat in a pan.
Fry the meat until golden brown.
Add rice.
Add salt, and pepper.
Cover and cook for 15–20 minutes.
Serve hot.
